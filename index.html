Featured Capability

AI that runs entirely
within your walls.

Most public AI services log your searches and use your conversations to train their models. For organizations and individuals handling sensitive information, this is an unacceptable risk. We deploy private AI systems that run on your own hardware—ensuring your enquiries remain your own, never shared with third-party providers.

  • ✓ Total Sovereignty: Conversations and searches never leave your network.
  • ✓ No Third-Party Training: Your data is never used to improve someone else's model.
  • ✓ Full LLM Capability: The power of GPT-class models, deployed locally or in private cloud.
  • ✓ Zero Compliance Risk: Architected for PIPEDA and absolute confidentiality.
  • ✓ Unlimited Internal Use: No per-query costs or API rate limits.

About

The studio
behind the work.

Just Me Media is an independent product development studio established in 2022, based in Minesing, Ontario, Canada. We design and build AI-powered software products and private AI infrastructure for clients who require capability without compromise.

Our portfolio spans consumer travel technology, sports engagement platforms, and B2B data APIs — each product conceived, built, and deployed to production as a fully operational service.

We take on a selective number of client engagements each year. We work with both established leadership teams and individuals seeking private, secure environments to transform an initial concept into a live, production-ready product. Currently available for project enquiries.
